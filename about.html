About Us

Catstone was established in 2021, with the idea of being a platform to help preloved pets find new caring homes around the country.

Through the power of html, css and javascript, we can connect you with your adoptive pet and hopefully bring them in to your home as a new and grateful member of the family.

We at Catstone are 6 people who've come together to create this project and inject a little goodness back into the world!
